Community

Amenities & Features

Coastal living elevated with resort-caliber amenities designed for the beach lifestyle.

Rooftop Terrace

Panoramic ocean views with lounge seating, a bar area, and sunset gathering space.

Surf Storage

Secure board racks with rinse stations so you can go straight from the waves to your door.

EV Charging

Level 2 charging stations in the garage — charge overnight, road-trip by morning.

Ocean-View Gym

Floor-to-ceiling windows facing the Pacific with Pelotons, free weights, and TRX rigs.

Infinity Pool

Heated saltwater pool with ocean-edge vanishing lip and sun loungers.

Outdoor Yoga Deck

Open-air platform overlooking the coast with sunrise and sunset class programming.

Coffee Bar

Complimentary espresso, cold brew on tap, and a cozy lounge to start the morning right.

Bike Share Station

Complimentary cruiser bikes and e-bikes for exploring PCH and the Balboa Peninsula.

Fire Pit Lounge

Beachside-style fire pit with built-in seating — perfect for evening gatherings.

Beach Gear Checkout

Borrow paddleboards, boogie boards, beach chairs, umbrellas, and coolers — all complimentary for residents.
